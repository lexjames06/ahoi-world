Have you ever found yourself intrigued by puzzles, fascinated by problem-solving, or drawn to the challenge of fixing things? If so, coding might be the perfect path for you. As a software engineer who embarked on a career change in my 30s, I want to share my journey and explore why coding could be the right choice for those considering a career change or deciding on their field of study. Let's dive in and discover why coding might just be the opportunity you've been looking for.

![rubiks - the original puzzle](./signs-coding-might-be-for-you/rubiks.jpg)

### You Like Puzzles

If you enjoy puzzles, whether it's solving crosswords, sudoku, or brainteasers, you already possess a mindset that aligns with coding. Coding is like solving puzzles every day, where the challenge lies in finding elegant and efficient solutions to complex problems. The ability to break down problems into smaller, more manageable pieces and analyze them systematically is a skill that translates seamlessly into the world of coding. Embrace your puzzle-solving nature and embark on a coding journey where every line of code becomes a piece of a grand puzzle waiting to be solved.

### You Like Problem Solving in General

Beyond puzzles, if you find joy in tackling problems and finding innovative solutions, coding offers an expansive playground for your problem-solving skills. From developing algorithms to optimizing processes, coding allows you to solve real-world problems and make a tangible impact. As a coder, you'll encounter a myriad of challenges that require creative thinking, logical reasoning, and perseverance. Embrace the thrill of problem solving, and coding will open doors to a world of endless opportunities and intellectual stimulation.

![sudoku the ultimate problem solving game](./signs-coding-might-be-for-you/sudoku.jpg)

### You Like Fixing Things or Taking Things Apart

Do you have a natural curiosity about how things work? Are you the type of person who enjoys taking things apart to understand their inner workings? Coding provides you with a similar experience. Just as you would disassemble a gadget to explore its components, coding allows you to delve into the intricacies of software and systems. By understanding the inner mechanisms and building blocks of code, you gain the power to create, improve, and shape the digital world. The joy of fixing things is amplified as you become the architect of your own creations.

![lego pieces have so many possibilities](./signs-coding-might-be-for-you/lego.jpg)

### You Like Digital Autonomy

Coding grants you a unique form of autonomy in the digital realm. In today's technology-driven world, having the ability to build your own applications, websites, or software grants you independence and empowers you to bring your ideas to life. Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur or simply seeking control over your digital environment, coding offers you the freedom to create and customize according to your vision. Embrace the power of digital autonomy, and coding will become your passport to a world of innovation and self-expression.

### My Journey from Career Switcher to Engineer

I'm here speaking from experience; I'm someone who made a career change at the age of 30. With a background in mathematics and a diverse work history ranging from customer service to personal training, I stumbled upon coding and fell in love with its problem-solving nature. Despite having no prior experience, I took the leap, honed my coding skills, and built a successful career as a software engineer. Coding fulfilled my need for puzzles, ignited my passion for problem solving, and allowed me to combine my analytical mindset with creativity.

![my personal journey includes being a personal trainer](./signs-coding-might-be-for-you/fitness.jpg)

### The Takeaway

Coding opens doors to a world of endless possibilities for those considering a career change or searching for their field of study. If you enjoy puzzles, possess a natural problem-solving inclination, find joy in fixing things, or crave digital autonomy, coding might be the perfect fit for you. Embrace the challenge, unlock your potential, and embark on a journey that combines creativity, logic, and personal growth. Remember, it's never too late to pursue your passion and transform your life through coding.

If my journey resonates with you, take the first step and dive into the world of coding. You never know where it might lead you.

If you found this blog post inspiring, please share it with others who might be seeking a change or exploring their career options.
